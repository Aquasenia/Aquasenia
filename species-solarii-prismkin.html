Species dossier

Solarii Prismkin

Luminous crystal beings forged in harmony halls, sworn to guard ancient resonance sites.

Lifespan 240–300 stellar cycles
Average height 2.1 meters
Average weight 180 kilograms
Relationship with humans Peaceful
Species status Stable
Overview

The Solarii Prismkin are crystalline guardians whose bodies refract stellar light into protective glyphs. Their watchposts anchor the Obsidian Belt's peace accords.

Origin realm: Obsidian Belt

Era discovered: Resonance Era

Primary role: Guardians

Key traits
  • Crystalline bodies that pulse with prismatic light.
  • Guardians of harmony halls and resonance keys.
  • Skilled in glyphwork and protective lattice weaving.

Biology & Anatomy

Prismkin bodies are lattices of living crystal that reconfigure under pressure, refracting light into adaptive shields and resonant wards for nearby allies.

  • Light refraction patterns are unique to each guardian lineage.
  • Core shards store ancestral memories and vow-echoes.
  • Resonance pulses act as a shared alert system across watchposts.

Deities & Religions

Solarii faith centers on the Everlight Chorus, a pantheon of stellar harmonics embodied in light rites performed within the harmony halls.

  • Ritual choirs align crystals to recalibrate sacred light lattices.
  • Resonance keys are treated as reliquaries of divine resonance.
  • Equinox Glow renewals honor the founding prism forges.

Background

Forged in the earliest resonance wars, the Prismkin were shaped to defend rift sanctums and keep the Obsidian Belt neutral in interstellar conflicts.

  • Founding treaties are etched into the walls of the first harmony halls.
  • Prism shields are lent to allied ships during major crossings.
  • Lineages keep oral logs of every guardian deployment.

Society & Roles

Every Prismkin is assigned a triad of duties that rotate across watchposts: protect the resonance keys, escort allied travelers, and mediate Belt disputes.

  • Guardian rotations are logged in the Obsidian Accord records.
  • Resonance medics mend fractured lattices with shared light.
  • Oaths are renewed during the Equinox Glow ritual.
