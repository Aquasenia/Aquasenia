Character dossier

Tarek of the Nine Lights

Diplomatic envoy who forged the Skybridge Accords and brokered interstellar peace.

Profile

Tarek earned the title of Nine Lights by weaving nine city-states into a single accord. His diplomacy remains a model for Crystal Age councils.

Lineage: Meralis

Era: Crystal Age

Signature focus: Treaty forging

Key notes
  • Architect of the Skybridge Accords during the Night of Ten Auroras.
  • Known for translating Mirror Sea treaty glyphs to starborne dialects.
  • Maintains envoy schools for emerging diplomats.
