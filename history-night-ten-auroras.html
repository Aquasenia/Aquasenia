The Night of Ten Auroras

The celestial convergence that ended the Astral Rift conflict and reshaped Li'Ona diplomacy.

Overview

During the Crystal Age, ten auroras aligned above the Mirror Sea, creating a window for peace talks. The Skybridge Accords were signed beneath the aurora canopy.

Era logged: Crystal Age

Primary attribute: Diplomacy

Archive code: HI-041

Key outcomes
  • Ended the Astral Rift conflict between the nine city-states.
  • Established the Skybridge Accords and diplomatic corridors.
  • Marked the rise of envoy guilds led by Tarek of the Nine Lights.

Prelude to peace

A convergence of resonance storms forced rival fleets to shelter under the same aurora canopy, giving diplomats a rare opportunity to negotiate without shifting battle lines.

  • Veyari pathfinders charted a temporary ceasefire corridor.
  • Meralis envoys brought tide-lens seals to verify intent.
  • Solarii guardians stabilized the Mirror Sea currents.

Ceremony of the ten lights

The accords were signed during a ritual where each city-state lit a beacon, creating a ring of ten luminous pillars reflected across the Mirror Sea.

  • Accords were recorded into crystal coral for archival permanence.
  • Envoy guilds were founded to sustain cross-system dialogue.
  • The first shared navigation atlas was commissioned.
