The Night of Ten Auroras

The celestial convergence that ended the Astral Rift conflict and reshaped Li'Ona diplomacy.

Overview

During the Crystal Age, ten auroras aligned above the Mirror Sea, creating a window for peace talks. The Skybridge Accords were signed beneath the aurora canopy.

Era logged: Crystal Age

Primary attribute: Diplomacy

Archive code: HI-041

Key outcomes
  • Ended the Astral Rift conflict between the nine city-states.
  • Established the Skybridge Accords and diplomatic corridors.
  • Marked the rise of envoy guilds led by Tarek of the Nine Lights.
