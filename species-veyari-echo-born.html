Veyari Echo-Born

Bio-luminescent navigators who translate resonance into star charts and chart the rifts between star currents.

Overview

The Veyari Echo-Born are revered for their attunement to sonic maps hidden in cosmic tides. Their lore centers on migration, harmonic diplomacy, and safeguarding the Resonant Codex.

Origin realm: Skybound Terraces

Era discovered: Founding Dawn

Primary role: Navigators

Key traits
  • Bio-luminescent skin patterns that shift with ambient resonance.
  • Navigation rituals performed through harmonic choral pulses.
  • Allied with the Skybridge Accords as neutral pathfinders.
