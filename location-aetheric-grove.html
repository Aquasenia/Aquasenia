The Aetheric Grove

Floating forests where sky-bloom trees sing with ambient light, forming a sanctuary above the Mirror Sea.

Overview

The Aetheric Grove is an airborne refuge tethered by luminous roots. Its groves host interstellar conclaves and serve as a waypoint for skybound travelers.

Region: Skybound Terraces

Era: Founding Dawn

Status: Active sanctuary

Highlights
  • Sky-bloom trees emit harmonic light trails for navigation.
  • Hosts the annual Resonance Bloom gathering.
  • Protected by Veyari Echo-Born pathfinders.
