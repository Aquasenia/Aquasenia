History entry

The Night of Ten Auroras

Summary

The Night of Ten Auroras was a Crystal Age convergence that ended the Astral Rift conflict and established the Skybridge Accords. Ten auroras aligned above the Mirror Sea, forcing rival fleets into a shared ceasefire window. The treaty signed that night reshaped Li'Ona diplomacy and opened stable travel corridors.

Classification / Type

History event; interstellar diplomatic convergence.

Key Facts

  • Era logged: Crystal Age.
  • Archive code: HI-041.
  • Primary attribute: Diplomacy.
  • Scale: Interstellar.
  • Ended the Astral Rift conflict between nine city-states.

Description

A convergence of resonance storms pushed rival fleets beneath the same aurora canopy. Veyari pathfinders charted a temporary ceasefire corridor while Meralis envoys and Solarii guardians stabilized the Mirror Sea currents for negotiations.

History

The accords were signed during a ceremony of ten luminous beacons reflected across the sea. Envoy guilds were founded afterward, and the first shared navigation atlas was commissioned to maintain open corridors.
