Citadel of Nine Currents

Summary

The Citadel of Nine Currents is a rotating city-state in the Obsidian Belt that balances tidal energy with ancient glyphwork. Its moving terraces host council chambers, market rings, and archive spires that adapt to incoming envoys. The citadel serves as a civic anchor for diplomacy and security across the region.

Classification / Type

Location; mobile citadel and council hub.

Key Facts

  • Region: Obsidian Belt.
  • Landmark type: Citadel.
  • Status: Active city-state.
  • Tide engine core realigns the city during rift drift.
  • Protected by Solarii Prismkin guardians.

Description

A ring of nine tide engines powers the citadel, syncing with Solarii resonance keys and luminous service canals. Rotating terraces reveal diplomacy halls, market promenades, and archive spires filled with live glyphwork.

History

Built during the Radiant Age, the citadel became the seat of the Nine Currents council. Its rotating stewardship model ensures allied lineages share control of movement and security protocols through resonance attunement rites.
