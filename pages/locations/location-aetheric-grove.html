The Aetheric Grove

Summary

The Aetheric Grove is a floating forest sanctuary above the Mirror Sea, tethered by luminous roots and sky-bloom trees. It serves as a waypoint for travelers, a neutral meeting ground for conclaves, and a living canopy of healing nectar. The Grove hums with harmonic light trails that guide arrivals through the clouds.

Classification / Type

Location; sky sanctuary and diplomatic waypoint.

Key Facts

  • Region: Skybound Terraces.
  • Landmark type: Sky sanctuary.
  • Status: Active sanctuary protected by rotating guardianship.
  • Hosts the annual Resonance Bloom gathering.
  • Known for luminous navigation trails in the canopy.

Description

Sky-bloom trees form layered platforms of bioluminescent leaves, creating corridors of light that shift with seasonal winds. Ringed terraces specialize in diplomacy, restoration, and study, while echo archives preserve migration songs for visiting crews.

History

Established during the Founding Dawn, the Grove became a neutral refuge for early Skybridge travelers. Over time it grew into a sanctuary network, with observatories monitoring aurora shifts and storm wards protecting the region during major gatherings.
