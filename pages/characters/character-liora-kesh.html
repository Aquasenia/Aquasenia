Character dossier

Liora Kesh

Summary

Liora Kesh is the chief archivist of the Resonant Codex and the steady voice of Li'Ona's memory vaults. She guides seekers through sensitive records, balances diplomacy with preservation, and keeps fragile echoes from destabilizing the archive. Her leadership sets the ethical standard for every archivist in the council.

Classification / Type

Character; Veyari archivist and council steward.

Key Facts

  • Role: Archivist and guardian of the Resonant Codex.
  • Era: Convergence Era.
  • Lineage: Veyari.
  • Signature focus: Codex preservation and resonance ethics.
  • Oversaw vaulting protocols after the Nebula Breach.

Description

Liora oversees the memory vaults beneath the Archive Index, coordinating nightly resonance audits and working with Prismkin guardians during vault resets. She also leads the ethics forum, teaching archivists how to handle sensitive memories with consent-driven rituals.

History

Following the Nebula Breach, Liora established the threefold consent rite and rebuilt the Codex preservation workflow. She now compiles an interspecies chronicle that weaves Meralis memory threads with Veyari resonance logs for future councils.
