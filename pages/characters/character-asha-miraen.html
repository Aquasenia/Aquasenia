Character dossier

Asha Miraen

Summary

Asha Miraen is a Solarii cartographer who maps the hidden paths between Li'Ona realms and rift corridors. Her Stellar Weave atlas guides expedition crews through shifting routes, and her calm fieldwork keeps rescue lanes open. She bridges archivists, Prismkin guardians, and Meralis diplomats when routes destabilize.

Classification / Type

Character; Solarii explorer and rift cartographer.

Key Facts

  • Role: Explorer.
  • Era: Radiant Age.
  • Lineage: Solarii.
  • Signature focus: Rift cartography and resonance tuning.
  • Developed the Stellar Weave atlas used by expedition crews.

Description

Trained in crystal observatories, Asha blends field observation with resonance tuning to mark safe corridors that shift each season. She uses prism beacons to triangulate unstable rifts and updates archival glyphs for navigation teams.

History

Descended from Solarii navigators who served the first Lumina Pact convoys, Asha built her reputation by decoding resonance storms along the Obsidian Belt. Her atlas is now the standard reference for emergent rift harmonics.
