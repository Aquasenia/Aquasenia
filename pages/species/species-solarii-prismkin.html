Species dossier

Solarii Prismkin

Summary

The Solarii Prismkin are luminous crystal beings forged in harmony halls to guard the Obsidian Belt. Their bodies refract stellar light into protective glyphs that shield allies during rift crossings. They serve as calm, steady guardians who protect resonance sites and keep peace accords intact.

Classification / Type

Species of crystalline guardians and glyph-weavers.

Key Facts

  • Lifespan spans 240–300 stellar cycles.
  • Average height is 2.1 meters; average weight is 180 kilograms.
  • Resonance pulses create shared alerts across watchposts.
  • Peaceful relationship with humans and allied fleets.
  • Primary role centers on harmony hall and rift sanctum defense.

Description

Prismkin bodies are lattices of living crystal that reconfigure under pressure, turning light into adaptive shields and resonant wards. Core shards store ancestral memories, while glyphwork keeps watchposts synchronized across the Belt.

History

Forged in the earliest resonance wars, the Prismkin were shaped to defend rift sanctums and keep the Obsidian Belt neutral. Their founding treaties remain etched into the first harmony halls and are renewed during Equinox Glow rituals.
