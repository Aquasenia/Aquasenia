Species dossier

Veyari Echo-Born

Summary

The Veyari Echo-Born are bio-luminescent navigators who read resonance patterns in the star currents. They live in the Skybound Terraces and chart safe routes through rift corridors for every allied fleet. Their communities are built on shared songs, calm diplomacy, and a living archive of travel chants.

Classification / Type

Species of resonance-guided navigators aligned with the Skybridge Accords.

Key Facts

  • Lifespan averages 150–190 sky cycles.
  • Average height is 1.8 meters with bioluminescent skin.
  • Navigation rituals rely on harmonic choral pulses.
  • Diplomatic status with humans remains neutral.
  • Primary role is charting resonance routes for shared travel.

Description

Veyari bodies store resonance in sub-dermal echo sacs, letting their luminescent patterns shift with every change in the star currents. Their gatherings feel like living songs, with elders guiding crews through call-and-response navigation rituals.

History

The Echo-Born emerged from early Skybound settlements during the Founding Dawn. They codified harmonic charts inside the Resonant Codex, opening safe trade lanes that later formed the backbone of the Skybridge Accords.
