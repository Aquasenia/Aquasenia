Species dossier

Veyari Echo-Born

Bio-luminescent navigators who translate resonance into star charts and chart the rifts between star currents.

Lifespan 150–190 sky cycles
Average height 1.8 meters
Average weight 68 kilograms
Relationship with humans Neutral
Species status Stable
Overview

The Veyari Echo-Born are revered for their attunement to sonic maps hidden in cosmic tides. Their lore centers on migration, harmonic diplomacy, and safeguarding the Resonant Codex.

Origin realm: Skybound Terraces

Era discovered: Founding Dawn

Primary role: Navigators

Key traits
  • Bio-luminescent skin patterns that shift with ambient resonance.
  • Navigation rituals performed through harmonic choral pulses.
  • Allied with the Skybridge Accords as neutral pathfinders.

Biology & Anatomy

Veyari bodies store resonance in sub-dermal echo sacs, letting their luminescent patterns shift in response to changing star currents.

  • Bio-luminescent skin aligns with harmonic flight songs.
  • Inner ear fins read micro-vibrations in rift corridors.
  • Echo sacs preserve lineage chants for navigation.

Deities & Religions

Their faith honors the Skybound Chorus, a constellation of guiding spirits who, they say, hum the safest paths through the rift currents.

  • Route singers offer first-light chants before any crossing.
  • Sky shrines are built atop terraces for nightly observances.
  • Festival skies fill with lanterns to honor lost voyagers.

Background

The Echo-Born emerged from early Skybound settlements as navigators, mapping resonance routes that allowed trade and diplomacy to expand across the rift corridors.

  • Peace routes are logged in the Resonant Codex for shared access.
  • Founding elders codified harmonic charts for future crews.
  • Joint expeditions reinforce trust with Solarii and Meralis crews.

Culture & Training

Veyari gatherings are orchestrated like living songs, with elders directing harmonic call and response to align travelers to the skies above the Skybound Terraces.

  • Choral councils set voyage blessings before every expedition.
  • Echo bands attune starships with rhythm-coded coordinates.
  • Rift simulators mirror storms to rehearse crisis navigation.
